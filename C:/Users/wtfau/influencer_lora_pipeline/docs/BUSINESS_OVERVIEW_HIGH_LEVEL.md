## Operations Overview

### Content Production Pipeline

```
1. Persona Design
   ↓
2. Training Data Collection (50-120 images)
   ↓
3. LoRA Training (Flux.1-dev)
   ↓
4. Prompt Engineering & Refinement
   ↓
5. Batch Generation (SFW/Spicy/NSFW lanes)
   ↓
6. Quality Control
   ↓
7. Vault Storage
   ↓
8. Scheduled Posting
```

**Generation Capacity:**
- Per image: 5-14 seconds
- Daily capacity: 2,400-4,000 images (80% uptime)
- Initial vault setup: 240 images in 25-60 minutes

### Customer Interaction Flow

```
1. Discovery (Social Media)
   ↓
2. Engagement (Likes, Comments)
   ↓
3. Funnel (Link in Bio)
   ↓
4. Conversion (OnlyFans Subscription)
   ↓
5. Retention (Chat, PPV, Tips)
   ↓
6. VIP (Custom Content, High Spending)
```

**CRM Integration:**
- Every interaction logged to Go High Level
- Customer profiles with preferences
- Engagement scoring (0-100)
- Automated segmentation
- Personalized communication

### Technology Stack

**Content Generation:**
- Flux.1-dev (base model)
- LoRA training per persona
- ComfyUI/Automatic1111 (backends)
- Claude/Grok (prompt enhancement)

**Orchestration:**
- Custom Python pipeline
- Persona manager
- Multi-lane content strategy
- Automated replenishment

**CRM & Data:**
- Go High Level (customer tracking)
- Discord bot (management interface)
- Analytics dashboards
- Revenue attribution

**Infrastructure:**
- Local GPU (initial): RTX 4090/A100
- Cloud compute (scaling): AWS/Azure
- Storage: Local NAS + cloud backup
- CDN: For content delivery

## Risk Analysis

### Critical Risks

**1. Platform Policy Changes**
- **Risk:** OnlyFans/Fansly ban AI content
- **Mitigation:** Diversify across 15+ platforms, don't over-concentrate
- **Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** High

**2. AI Detection & Disclosure**
- **Risk:** Platforms require AI disclosure, reducing appeal
- **Mitigation:** Transparency from start, position as "digital personas"
- **Likelihood:** High | **Impact:** Medium

**3. Content Quality/Uncanny Valley**
- **Risk:** AI-generated content doesn't convert
- **Mitigation:** Heavy QA, human-in-loop for chat, premium positioning
- **Likelihood:** Low | **Impact:** High

**4. Regulatory/Legal**
- **Risk:** New regulations around AI adult content
- **Mitigation:** Legal counsel, compliance monitoring, geographic diversification
- **Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

**5. Competition**
- **Risk:** Others copy model, race to bottom
- **Mitigation:** Speed, quality, data moat, brand differentiation
- **Likelihood:** High | **Impact:** Medium

### Operational Risks

**6. Technical Infrastructure**
- **Risk:** GPU failures, downtime
- **Mitigation:** Redundancy, cloud backup, SLAs
- **Likelihood:** Low | **Impact:** Medium

**7. Payment Processing**
- **Risk:** Chargebacks, account holds
- **Mitigation:** Multiple processors, reserves, excellent service
- **Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** Low

**8. Scaling Challenges**
- **Risk:** Cannot maintain quality at 400 profiles
- **Mitigation:** Test extensively at 200 before expanding
- **Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium
