- **Likelihood:** Medium (30-50%)
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Diversify across 21 platforms
  - Some platforms explicitly allow AI
  - International platforms less restrictive

**T2: Market Saturation**
- **Likelihood:** High (inevitable)
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Niche differentiation
  - Premium positioning
  - First-mover advantage

**T3: Technological Disruption**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Stay current with latest AI models

**T4: Regulatory Changes**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** Medium-High
- **Mitigation:**

  - Geographic diversification

**T5: Economic Downturn**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**

  - Affordable pricing
  - Multiple price tiers
